In this increasingly challenging business environment, there are businesses who have successfully made the difference thanks to excellent strategies and practices. These NEW RACE NEW WAY spirit and extraordinary achievements deserve the finest recognition, and these success stories will inspire business community creating more sustainable values.

Anphabe’s Certification Program VIETNAM EXCELLENCE honors leaders and businesses who demonstrated excellence and impact across the organizations in transforming business through talent & workplace development. In return, they will also serve as the role models and accelerate their betterment through practice, knowledge sharing and leadership mentoring for business community.
