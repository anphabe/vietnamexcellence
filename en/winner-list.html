Visionary Leader

Ms. VƯU LỆ QUYÊN

Chief Executive Officer - BITI'S

In the era of volatilities and uncertainties, businesses must change to adapt. As one of the longest standing enterprises in Vietnam with nearly 40 years in operation, Biti’s had to transform itself to survive and grow. What strategies did Biti’s follow? Being one of the youngest CEOs of the 10,000-worker corporation, Ms. Vuu Le Quyen has chosen to adapt by completely changing Biti’s business: from working culture, operating processes, to product designs and marketing and communication visions. She chose to be a compassionate leader who always puts the happiness and well-being of her community first while maintaining its performance. Under her leadership and the full support of her colleagues, Biti’s has had a spectacular transformation, bringing impressive business results and positive values in the past few years.

Visionary Leader

Mr. NITIN KAPOOR

Chairman and General Director - AstraZeneca Vietnam

Under Nitin's leadership, AstraZeneca Vietnam has successfully transformed its business and strengthened its workplace culture, generating an outstanding performances despite industry challenges. The company has championed public-private partnerships with the Vietnamese Government and healthcare partners on various meaningful initiatives to reduce the non-communicable diseases burden and promote sustainability, while supporting the country’s fight against COVID-19 with its vaccine supply.

The company’s efforts were recognised with two award certificates from the Minister of Health in 2019 and 2020 for its contributions to Vietnam’s fight against cancer and COVID-19, a Medal from the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union for outstanding impact on Vietnamese youths, and Vietnam Top 100 Places to Work in 2018 - 2020.

Visionary Leader

Mr. WARRICK CLEINE

Chairman and CEO of KPMG in Vietnam and Cambodia

Mr. Warrick Cleine is the Chairman and CEO of KPMG in Vietnam and Cambodia. He has been an advisor in Vietnam for over 20 years, and is recognised as one of Vietnam’s leading tax professionals. He has extensive knowledge and experience in Vietnam of taxation advisory and compliance, tax dispute resolution, corporate governance and structuring issues, and has advised on numerous corporate transactions in Vietnam and Cambodia.

Warrick is well known and respected by the corporate community in Vietnam, and has extensive experience working with the authorities at all levels of the Government. He is a frequent commentator in the Vietnamese media on tax, governance and business issues.

Outside of KPMG, Mr. Cleine is Chairman of the New Zealand Chamber of Commerce in Vietnam, Chairman of British Corporate Advocacy Council in Vietnam, and Adjunct Industry Professor in the School of Business & Management, RMIT Vietnam University.

Visionary Leader

Mrs. NGUYỄN ĐỨC THẠCH DIỄM

Member of the Board of Directors cum CEO - Sai Gon Thuong Tin Commercial Joint Stock Bank - SACOMBANK

Starting her career at Sacombank as a credit officer in 2002 and continued to hold various positions, Ms. Diem has made tremendous contributions to Sacombank over the last 18 years. She is well known as a decisive and brave leader, especially in problem-solving.

Reviewing some of her main milestones before she leads the Board of Directors (BOD) of Sacombank, Ms. Diem was the Executive in charge of South Central & Western Highlands Region (2013 – 2016) and made it the leading business of Sacombank system, where the market is defined as competitive and tough.

In May 2017, Ms. Diem became CEO of Sacombank, and later in April 2018, she was enlisted as a Member of BOD. Under her leadership, she has maintained effective performance and got Sacombank back on track, at the same time quickly resolving issues after M&A (mainly focusing on bad debt resolution and restructuring all aspects of operation).
